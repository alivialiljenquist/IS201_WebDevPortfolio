Recently Watched

My last three movies, ranked in the order I watched them:

  1. Disclosure Day
    • Rating: 2/5 stars
    • A forgettable sci-fi thriller that was supposed to be a fun watch but was just boring. Steven Spielberg didn't deliver this time. I was expecting more of a thriller and less of a car chase. They should have made the last half of the movie be the whole movie.
  2. Backrooms
    • Rating: 4/5 stars
    • Genuinely unsettling. The liminal-space horror works because it commits to freaky dread over jump scares — flickering fluorescent lights, endless yellow hallways, and the growing sense that something is wrong with reality itself. It's a fun watch, but not for the faint of heart.
  3. Masters of the Universe
    • Rating: 2/5 stars
    • Nostalgia cannot save this one. The campy 80s fantasy feels dated rather than charming, with stiff performances and a plot that exists mainly to sell action figures. Skeletor almost makes it fun — almost.
